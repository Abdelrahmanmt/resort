Golf Course Tri duby

A professional 18-hole course just 5 km from the hotel. Accommodated guests of Hotel & Spa Resort Kaskady can enjoy a 50% discount from current Green fee prices. There is also a golf school for beginners with a professional coach. The green card course for a special price of € 300 lasts 12 hours.

http://www.tridubygolf.sk
Summer activities
Golf course Gray Bear Tále

The first 18-holes championship golf course in Slovakia is located 57 km away from the hotel. The scenery of the mountain valley is rich in natural beauty and its natural landmarks are the stream bed, rocky boulders, massive fir tree and pines. Design of 6296 m long course is clear, all holes are correct despite the fact that their demands require more precise gaming humility and irons game. Guest of the Hotel Kaskady may use a 10% discount from the current green fee.

http://www.golfklubtale.sk

Summer activities
Mining educational path in Špania Dolina

This path introduces the mining history of Špania Dolina and takes you to the place of its past glory hidden in the surrounding forests. It starts at the Klopačka and from this point it passes during the rising path along the shafts and adits to the scenic view on the heap Maximilian with a wonderful panoramic view on the surrounding mountains. Walking the path with ten stops will take you about two and a half hours, the excess is about 70 m and its intensity is low and suitable for families with children.
Summer activities
Educational path boky

Do you know where you can find Devil’s Rock? If you did not hear about it, feel free to visit Natural Reservation Boky which is located closely to the Zvolen, near the village Budča. The path which is 6 km long passes through the reservation and includes 13 panels, on which the visitor can find information about the characteristics of animals, plants, geomorphologic natural formation. Waking this path takes you 5 hours.

http://www.chkopolana.eu
Summer activities
Educational path králická tiesňava – waterfall

Path passes through natural monument – canyon called Králická tiesňava, which includes a beautiful 7 meter colored waterfall. In this location are numerous protected species of flora and fauna. Path starts from the village Králiky near Banská Bystrica.

Summer activities
Rafting

Enjoy adventure on the river Hron. You can raft from the city Brezno to the city Zvolen and far away if you choose a day or more in the water. Rafting on the river, where you will experience great entertainment and right canoeing atmosphere with a touch of adrenaline is an excellent opportunity for building the friendships and improving the labor collectives. Your reward will be the unforgettable experience. Boathouse on Mlynčok in the city Slovenská Ľupča ensures complete technical support for the rafting on river Hron, including lending and transporting of the equipment and ships. On the boathouse you have several options of sports such as beach volleyball, table tennis, petanque, for children are available climbing frames, swings, climbing wall and sand. Boathouse offers refreshing in the cafeteria and accommodation in the campground.

http://www.splavhrona.sk
Summer activities
Lunter's ranch

In the recreation area Kráľová located 8 km from Zvolen with beautiful countryside ideal for riding, is situated Lunters´ranch. Ranch offers western riding for all age categories and levels of riding. The most frequent visitors are families with children and the ranch is open for all visitors 24 hours a day.

http://www.lunterovranc.sk
Summer activities
Farm Marekov dvor

Family farm in the area called Vrchy which includes three municipalities: Sihla, Lom nad Rimavicou and Drábsko in the district Brezno. Farm offers riding services, production of the milk and cheese and farm with small animals.

http://www.marekovdvor.sk
Summer activities
Holidaypark Kováčová

A newly built modern year-round resort with thermal mineral water. During the summer season you can take a refreshing dip in the outdoor swimming pool, in the winter you can warm up yourself in the inner part with a large whirlpool, children’s pool and relaxation pool with hydromassage elements. You can have fun riding the 60 m long water slide.

http://www.holidaypark.sk
Summer activities
Paintball

Adrenaline fun for all age categories and for all people tired of sitting at home. It is a non-contact sport in which physical strength and stamina are irrelevant, but the ability to think and react quickly is necessary. Paintball is a modern, dynamic and strategic game. Despite the fact that there are many ways of this game, it is played by two teams against each other in order to neutralize the opponent’s players using paintball balls fired from paintball guns. This game is played in an interesting location Sielnica in the area of the former cooperatives, it has its own playground with an area more than 8000 m2, where are 2 buildings and a number of obstacles in the natural terrain.
