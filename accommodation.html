Hotel & Spa Resort Kaskady—
Kaskady inclusive services

Come and enjoy what we've prepared for you.

To make your stay tasty

Breakfast buffet

Rich breakfast buffet at the hotel restaurant from 6:30 to 10 am, in July and August from 07 to 10:30 am.

Loyalty program

  • 15% discount on catering services with Gold loyalty profile.
  • 10% discount on catering services with Silver loyalty profile.
For beautiful days as well as nights
  • Comfortable and air-conditioned rooms, suites and villas in a four-star standard without a balcony, with balcony or terrace, bath or shower, WC, hairdryer, safe, satellite TV, radio, minibar, telephone
  • In all rooms guests find the SUNDARI cosmetic, whose products are made of pure natural essences, contain no artificial dyes and its production is based on Ayurvedic principles
  • In all rooms is also Cascade cosmetic with a pleasant fresh smell and beautiful design
  • Bathrobe and slippers
  • Coffee and tea service

Loyalty program

  • 15% discount on accommodation services with Gold loyalty profile.
  • 10% discount on accommodation services with Silver loyalty profile.
Place for relaxation of your body and soul

Unlimited access to Wellness & Spa with an area of 3,000 m², including:

  • Indoor swimming pool with thermal water. From May to September, guests can use outdoor pool with sun loungers, beach baskets and beach umbrellas, and access to the Natural Spa
  • BLUE SPA – steam sauna, Finnish sauna, infrared sauna, ice fall, tepidarium, jacuzzi, swimsuit spinner
  • GOLD SPA – steam sauna, Finnish sauna, infrared sauna, herbal sauna, mint sauna, eucalyptus sauna, ice fall, tepidarium, jacuzzi, swimsuit spinner, relaxation zone (entry from 18 years)
  • Natural Spa – outdoor jacuzzi, outdoor cooling pool, children´s pool, outdoor relaxation area
  • Free access to the fitness center, badminton, beach playground, 3D archery (only with the animator), outdoor tennis courts

Loyalty program

  • 20% discount on wellness services with Gold loyalty profile.
  • 20% discount on wellness services with Silver loyalty profile.
For children´s guests
  • Baby cot on request for free
  • Baby stool, changing desk
  • Children´s play area with children´s films
  • A game room with the ability to play multimedia games and X-box
  • Possibility to borrow parlour games
  • Children´s playground (slides, climbing frames, swings, sandpit)
  • Children´s menu in the restaurant, or diet food
  • Outdoor children´s pool
  • Bathrobes for children, possibility to buy baby nappies, sleeves
  • For swimming or floating wheel
  • Children´s step, children´s set (crayons, paintings, balloons)
  • Rich animation program for children during selected weekends and holidays
  • Securing the childcare (after prior arrangement at least one day before the stay, depending on actual occupancy)
Important details
  • Parking (for more than 12 hours for fee)
  • WiFi connection in the whole hotel
  • Shoe cleaner
  • Safe at reception
  • Luggage storage
  • Bikes storage
  • Sports equipment rental – tennis rackets, balls, bicycles, e-bikes on request
