About accommodation

Stylish and comfortably furnished suite in newly built accommodation in 2017 with separate bedroom with double bed, living area with sofa bed for 1 person or 2 children up to 12 years (140 x 200), minibar, kettle set for tea and coffee, ironing set in the room, balcony, WIFI, TV SAT, bathroom with shower, air conditioning, bathrobe and slippers available room.

The rooms are non-smoking.

A Kaskady inclusive service included as our attention for your wonderful stay.Included breakfast and unlimited access to the thermal pools and the Wellness & Spa.

Information

Child to 2,99 years old is gratis.
The price does not include the local tax 1,50 € / night / person.
